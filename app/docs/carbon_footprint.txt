Reducing your carbon footprint involves changing daily habits to lower greenhouse gas emissions.
Opt for public transport or carpooling instead of driving alone.
Use energy-efficient appliances like refrigerators and washing machines with a high star rating.
Eating less meat and dairy can significantly cut down your personal carbon output.
Avoid fast fashion and choose durable, ethically-made clothing.
Offset your footprint by planting trees or supporting renewable energy projects.
